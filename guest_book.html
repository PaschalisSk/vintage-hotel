Guest Book

Read what some of our guests had to say about their stay in our hotel.

Here are some of our post collection from Tripadvisor, Booking.com and Hotels.com

“Lovely, fab boutique hotel”

What a great place! Couldn't fault it. Great interiors, excellent customer service, loved my room- comfortable beds, clean bright bathroom with NUXE toiletries and breakfast was delicious! I can't fault it and I would recommended this place for families, couples and friends.

“Different”

Very distinctive Hotel. Comfortable and fun. Good living space and informal service. Adds to the travel experience and provides a special touch. Good for families. In my case heating system unsufficient in cold weather bringing a certain degree of uncomfort especially during the night. Thats why 3 stars ilo 4.

“Excellent Hotel”

We stayed here for a week for my husband business trip..Excellent place to stay! comfortable, clean and very convenient location near the tourist places or downtown.. very close to many shops and restaurants. All staff were courteous and welcoming. Excellent customer service and nice rooms and decorations (very vintage :) Augustin and Ornina was an amazing staff in vintage hotel... my favorite part is breakfast which i enjoyed eating their delicious bread.. :)

“Charming place to stay in brussels”

Vintage hotel has a good location in between the centre (Marolles Antique place) and the trendy neighbourhoods of St Gilles and Ixelles.It is a cosy place with a nice vintage decoration indeed. The staff is friendly

Stayed April 2016, traveled with friends

“Quirky hotel - nice stay”

We put friends up in this hotel and they loved its vintage design. We had breakfast together and enjoyed it so much. It's close to Avenue Louise, close to a selection of very good restaurants and near public transport. Out of the ordinary, quirky place to stay.

“A favourite in Brussels”

Equal favorite with Zoom Hotel, same concept, original, clean, super friendly staff, and excellent breakfast. Although near Avenue Louise, the hotel is not only affordable but also very quiet and guarantees a good night sleep. Note that there is no airco so perhaps in summer it can get hot. There a little patio in front of the hotel with a 70s style trailer for those who want to spend a night in an even more original manner. The breakfast was good, the 70s theme throughout the hotel is great. The bedroom was not huge but sufficient for a couple of nights, very comfortable bed and clean. Good wifi access in the room.

Stayed April 2016, traveled on business
