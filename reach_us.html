Location

How to get there?

FROM THE AIRPORT (ZAVENTEM)

The Zaventem National Airport is 15 km away; or 15-20 minutes by Metro, Tram or Taxi.

By taxi, the fare to or from the Brussels city center is about €45. By train (Airport City Express), a single ticket costs €3-5 depending on the category. Click for full itinerary.


FROM THE THE SOUTH STATION

The South Station (Zuid) is the arrival station of the TGV, Thalys and Eurostar trains, and is just 3 km and 10 minutes by taxi from the hotel.

By metro, get off at "Louise" station: we are located 300 meters from the exit. Click for full itinerary
