Decoration

The rooms

We offer rooms with decoration inspired from the 70s but with a flat-screen cable TV.

Our rooms all feature a desk, a modern bathroom and an iHome docking station for both music players and radio alarm clocks.

Room facilities: safe, iron, desk, fan, heating system, hair dryer, toiletries, toilets, bathrooms (bath or shower), telephone, radio.

Free Wi-Fi connection is available throughout the hotel.

Hotel

Housed in a beautiful red brick building, dating from the early 20th century and with the typical Brussels architecture. The Vintage Hotel welcomes you with a quiet and sunny patio-terrace, ideal to sip on a sweet treat from the Wine Bar in the evenings after spending the day exploring Brussels, the nearby Avenue Louise and its area dedicated to shopping.

Offering a cosy Lounge, a breakfast room beautifully decorated and a Glamping experience renowned throughout Europe, the Vintage Hotel was awarded the "Visit Brussels Award" in 2012.
