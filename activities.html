Activities

Breakfast in Brussels

Your stay at the Vintage Hotel will give you a gourmet breakfast. In a designer setting and a relaxed atmosphere, to the sound of old vinyl records from the 60s and 70s, you will enjoy the fresh produce from our partner craftsmen (Fleur du Pain, Charly Juices, traditional jams, Dolfin chocolate, Palais des Thés, Segafredo, Restofrais, etc.). On the menu: sourdough baguettes, pure butter pastry, raisin bread, rye bread, cheese and pork platter, cereals, yoghurt, jams, fresh fruit and fruit salad, boiled eggs. Espresso, cappuccino, hot chocolate, teas and fruit juices

Vintage wine bar

Our Boutique Wine Bar is directed by Tim Vandeput which is "in da place". Not every day, but almost. Best Sommelier of Belgium in 2009, the opening year of the Vintage Hotel. Tim remains involved in the adventure of the Vintage Wine Bar, continuing to advise you about his finest wine discoveries from around the world (and not just from our French neighbours ;)), for deliciously tasted glasses from €6.

This selection mainly includes wines from small farmers from the four corners of the world. Wines with character and personality. Wines that can be enjoyed alone or with friends in an exceptional setting.

The Vintage Wine Bar is open daily.
